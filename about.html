MultiMain WaqtX shows you how much of your life you've already used — in days, heartbeats, and moments. Not to create fear, but to create focus.

What is MultiMain WaqtX?

MultiMain WaqtX is a life awareness tool built to make you think differently about time. Most people go through life without ever stopping to measure it. MultiMain WaqtX changes that.

Enter your date of birth and instantly see: how many days you've lived, how many you have left, your life progress as a percentage, your time twin (a famous person who shares your birthday), historical events from your birth date, and much more.

It's not a calculator. It's a reality check.
