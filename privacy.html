Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

MultiMain WaqtX is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal data belongs to you — not us.

✅ MultiMain WaqtX does NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any server. Everything runs entirely in your browser.

What data do we collect?

None. MultiMain WaqtX does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your date of birth
  • We do not collect your name
  • We do not require account creation or sign-up
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not send any data to external servers

Local storage

MultiMain WaqtX uses your browser's localStorage to remember your date of birth and name between visits. This data:

  • Stays entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted anywhere
  • Can be cleared at any time using the ✕ button on the profile welcome card
  • Is automatically cleared when you clear your browser data

Third-party services

MultiMain WaqtX uses the following third-party resources:

  • html2canvas (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — used only when you download a share card. No data is sent.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website. GitHub may log standard server access logs (IP address, browser type). See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Analytics

MultiMain WaqtX currently does not use any analytics tracking. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

Children's privacy

MultiMain WaqtX does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The tool is designed for general audiences and does not require any personal data submission.
